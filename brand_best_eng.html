Рализовано в новостройках Москвы и Московской области

BEST-Novostroy is ranked among the largest agencies in Moscow Region in terms of apartment sales in newly-constructed residential buildings. The company was founded in 2009. Today, its project-portfolio sales volumes amount to 1.6 million sq. m.

The company is known for its active engagement in the development and promotion of innovations in the real estate market, as well as for its commitment to creating state-of-the-art concepts for residential areas with their own infrastructure and living environment.

BEST-Novostroy pays much attention to staff training. In 1997, Irina Dobrokhotova, Chairperson of the company’s Board of Directors, initiated the establishment of Moscow’s first School for the Professional Development of Realtors and a consulting partner company, Best-Training. Through the direct involvement of Irina Dobrokhotova, 38 residential buildings were constructed in various districts of the capital city.

Currently, BEST-Novostroy’s team of professionals includes 200 residential-property market experts who provide services to customers, investors and developers.

In 2012, BEST-Novostroy successfully launched several milestone housing projects located in Moscow, Novaya Moskva and Moscow region. Apartment sales began in the large-scale residential area Novye Vatutinki, which attracted numerous property buyers and became one of the main attractions in Novaya Moskva. The company participated in development of the concept for the Novoye Butovo residential area and became an exclusive partner in its implementation. In the fall of 2012, BEST-Novostroy brought to market an absolutely unparalleled project – business-class residential quarters 9-18 in Mytishchi with their own infrastructure and European architecture.

Irina Evgenyevna Dobrokhotova
Chairman, Board of Directors, BEST-Novostroy
Birthday: 19 june

In 2009, Irina Dobrokhotova and her partners founded BEST-Novostroy Company. The main thrusts of her business endeavors included real estate investments, strategic consulting and sales management. Due to the creation of an effective business structure with powerful growth potential, the company managed in less than 2 years to bring together the best professionals on the real estate market and become one of top three companies with the most new-housing sales. In 2011, the company emerged victorious from the URBANAWARDS in the nomination for the best Realtor/Consultant of the Year 2011.

Ms. Dobrokhotova united young, bold and ambitious professionals who had already gained the trust of their customers and their construction-company partners. This was made possible due to the creative formulation of project concept, new innovative solutions, special approaches to client and developer communication, and the selfless dedication of staff to their work.

In her 18 successful years in real estate, Irina Dobrokhotova has conceived and implemented dozens of unique projects. Thanks to her professionalism and thorough expertise in her field, she has earned recognition and respect among market leaders, and the dedication of her staff. Irina Dobrokhotova has been a keynote speaker at popular conferences, meetings and presentation sessions. She is a frequent guest on radio and television. She is also one of the most widely-cited speakers in leading business mass media.

As an instructor and participant, she completed more than 200 training seminars on business strategy and development, project management, sales structuring and management, organizational management and many others.
